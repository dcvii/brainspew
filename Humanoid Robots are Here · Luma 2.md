About Event

Robots have moved beyond factory automation to humanoid robots that will be available for purchase in 2026. Rather than the clunky Robot Butler from TV shows, humanoid robots hold the potential to be the low-cost Butler, Security Guard, Gardener, Mechanic, Nurse and Handyman. New robots will be available from many companies this year to enable new markets and new business models for entrepreneurs.

Join us for an examination of this huge new market and its current advances with a Keynote Speakers, a panel discussion of experts and plenty of networking afterward.
