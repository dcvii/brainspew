## Watch how this small chopper flown inside a C-130 transport plane was used to rescue the U.S. aviator trapped in Iran after his F-15 was shot down

The military’s “Little Bird” helicopters played a key role in rescuing a U.S. airman who was trapped in the mountains of Iran after his jet fighter was shot down. The helicopters were flown into the country on transport planes and quickly assembled on the ground before completing the daring rescue.

Watch this video to learn more about the small aircraft flown by the Army’s 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment, commonly known as the “Night Stalkers.”
