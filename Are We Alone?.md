The blockade is coming down. President Trump announced over the weekend that a deal had been reached with Iran, and he’s authorizing the immediate removal of the U.S. Navy’s blockade of the Strait of Hormuz. Sal Mercogliano rejoins Mooch to break down what the announcement actually means — whether this is a genuine off-ramp from Operation Epic Fury or political signaling ahead of the next round of negotiations. The details of any Iran deal will matter enormously, and Sal is exactly the right person to read the shipping and maritime implications in real time.

Also this week: Commander Dave “Sex” Fravor talks with Mooch about the 2004 Nimitz UAP encounter and what two decades of additional evidence have done to his thinking about what’s out there and what the government knows. And Mooch digs into the newly released CSIS report on Pentagon munitions inventories — the concern that recent operations have drawn stocks down to dangerous levels — and how the Trump administration is responding to the alleged crisis. A lot to unpack in one issue.
