Keybase is owned by Keybase Inc. and founded by Maxwell Krohn (the Co-Founder of OKCupid) and Chris Coyne (the Co-Founder of OKCupid and SparkNotes.com).

The lead investor of Keybase is the venture capital firm - Andreessen Horowitz, which has invested in companies like Stripe, Instagram, Github, Business Insider, Dollar Shave Club, Lyft, Zynga, CryptoKitties, Kickstarter, Pinterest, Foursquare, Stack Overflow and others.

Other investors include:

- Corazon Capital - who have invested in projects like Authy.
- The Stellar Foundation
- Adam Ludwin - the founder and CEO of Chain which was acquired by The Stellar Foundation and renamed to Interstellar.
- Alexis Ohanian - co-founder of Reddit, ex-Product Manager at Conde Nast, husband to Serena Williams and Managing Partner of Initialized Capital which has invested in Origin Protocol, Cloud9, Horizon Blockchain Games, CoinTracker, Patreon and more.
- Bre Pettis - founder of Bold Machines and investor of Dash Labs.
- Christian Rudder - Co-founder of SparkNotes and OKCupid, and Venture Partner at Corazon Capital.
- Martin Casado - General Partner of Andreessen Horowitz and co-founder of Nicira Networks which was acquired by VMware.
- Sam Yagan - Co-founder of Corazon Capital, OkCupid and SparkNotes, investor of TradingView, ex-CEO of Match Group (which own Tinder, POF, OkCupid, Match.com and others).
- Tom Pinckney - VP at Ebay and Hunch whos investors have funded a *ton* of companies and projects like Cyanogen, Discord, Docker, Dropbox, Instagram, MySQL, MetaCafe, New Relic, SeekingAlpha, Snapchat, Twitter, Uber, Yelp, Xapo and more.
