## Keybase - About the company

Keybase is an acquired company based in New York City (United States), founded in 2013 by [Chris Coyne](https://platform.tracxn.com/a/d/people/52ffbb13e4b023b1c59cacf5/chriscoyne) and [Maxwell Krohn](https://platform.tracxn.com/a/d/people/53da0377e4b0fa22152d2816/maxwellkrohn). It operates as a Provider of identity mapping and secure messaging solutions with public keys. Keybase has raised $10.8M in funding. The company has 190 active competitors, including 6 funded and 4 that have exited. Its top competitors include companies like [Telegram](https://tracxn.com/d/companies/telegram/__x5J3nC5XHhI2U2R37tGoTUE-khzlZrR-RASqNMoarow), [Siilo](https://tracxn.com/d/companies/siilo/__Uwn51fufqENvLEt5QhKRTwV0jCZhNFV-bX0BMgsqdQ8) and [Session](https://tracxn.com/d/companies/session/__x-egueSpKuZ0t_HtHX9nPvWt5kPO81L-vZk_kr3UJg0).
