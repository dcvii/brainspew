I think it's preposterous to champion the idea that race realism is necessary to defeat wokism when it is demonstrably true that political and social indoctrination based on dubious premises is what created wokism in the first place. It's a good idea to consider the mental health of individuals supportive of any subculture - the subculture of wealthy white cocaine users, for example - would certainly be of more telling than to default to one drop rules of racialism. Do the truly intelligent and gifted individuals never use drugs? Never ruin themselves or their families because of drugs or alcohol? Is there some genetic predisposition in Jews that keep them from having addictive personalities? What about Quakers? I think Confas is too dismissive of the psychological attraction to functional or dysfunctional cultures, which is to be expected if stereotype realism asks everything of race and nothing more of mental health and culture.
