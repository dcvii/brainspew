## About

## Decades in design and technology

We’ve been working on the web (and often, together) since the late 1990s. We’ve seen and done enough to know what lasts and how to build it—efficiently and effectively. We’re as committed to upholding high standards as we are to doing whatever it takes to get the job done.

## Our collected story

Our experience comes from the work we’ve done, the lessons we’ve learned, and the friends we’ve made along the way.

- 2023
	#### Business
	**The Radavist** —The Radavist goes independent once again. As part of that transition, Faculty builds the Rad Bazaar.
- 2022
	#### Business
	**Matter** —Faculty does branding, strategy, and early product work for a neuroscience company that helps people live longer, healthier, happier lives.
	![Matter Team](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-matter-team.jpg)
- 2021
	#### Business
	**Faculty** —Emerging from the pandemic, we do good work for numerous clients, including [Keybase](https://faculty.com/work/keybase) and [Jessica Hische](https://faculty.com/work/jessica-hische).
- 2020
	#### Sponsor
	**New Adventures** —Faculty sponsors the fifth and final edition of the renowned UK design conference.
	![Supporting New Adventures](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-na-1.jpg)
- 2019
	#### Sponsor
	**New Adventures** —Faculty sponsors the return of the renowned UK design conference.
	![Supporting New Adventures](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-na-2.jpg)
- 2019
	#### Press
	**In Net Mag** —Simon Collison spots us in the magazine in his local newsagent.
	![In Net mag](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-colly-netmag.jpg)
- 2017
	#### Activity
	**Faculty gifts** —Starting a beloved yearly tradition. Each holiday season, we send our clients, partners, and friends some of the best bespoke gifts we can curate or create.
	![Faculty care package](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-gifts.jpg)
- 2017
	#### Activity
	**Faculty dinner** —To celebrate the official launch of Faculty, we hosted an intimate dinner party in downtown Boulder, CO.
- 2016
	#### Business
	**Faculty** —The Faculty team begins working on Samsung while getting the company off the ground.
- 2016
	#### Activity
	**Backcountry Club** —Chris and Chris go backpacking in Colorado and discuss working together.
	![Backcountry Club](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-backcountry1.jpg)
- 2015
	#### Coworking
	**Roost** —Chris Shiflett launches Roost, a coworking studio that’s home to a bunch of friendly creative people.
- 2014
	#### Activity
	**Web Camp (Montana)** —A small group of web luminaries go backpacking to unplug and talk about the future.
	![Web Camp Montana](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-webcamp1.jpg)
- 2011
	#### Confernce
	**Greenville Grok** —Grok is a roundtable unconference for independent thinkers who make our digital world better. Designers, developers, writers, strategists, project managers; if you create things online, you’re our kind of person.
- 2010
	#### Conference
	**Brooklyn Beta** —It began as a conference, but became more of a community, a place for the “work hard and be nice to people” crowd. Five editions from 2010 to 2014.
	![Brooklyn Beta](https://faculty.com/assets/images/content/timeline/tl-bb.png)
- 2007
	#### Coworking
	**Atlas Local** —A small group of independents and technologists grow tired of working from home, coffee shops, and cubicles, so they band together to start one of the very first coworking spaces. Many of them are still working together to this day.
