### Sick of Jew-hatred

I don’t even argue with anti-Semites anymore. I just say, “Don’t fuck with God’s Chosen People!” This, of course, drives them nuts, for all the right reasons: God, ancient covenants, the Bible, historical records, etc.

Anti-Semites love to compliment themselves; they suppose they have a point of view, an argument, a side to argue. Nope, sorry. Anti-Semitism is a kind of pre-murder, as Ben Hecht wrote long ago.

Words and actions go together, as we know, like night and day, brother and sister, beans and rice. When you release anti-Jew talk into public discourse, nothing good happens, only bad shit happens. And while we’re at it, let’s flush all the *elegant variations* down the toilet: *O I’m not anti-Semitic I’m just anti-Zionist*, or *AIPAC is out of control*, or, *I’m just against genocide*; if these come out of your lips you’re a Jew-hater. Straighten up, fly right. We must call anti-Semitism out - in whatever manifestation it occurs. I always held a secret pride that America treated Jewish people with equanimity more than other countries; that equanimity seems to be fraying at the edges.

And Europe. You’d think a continent that spent half a decade murdering Jews would make a few conciliatory gestures towards Israel along with a few admonitory gestures towards Iran, the country that wants to bomb Israel into nothing, while rattling the world through its geography and oil. America has invested huge amounts of blood and treasure keeping Europe from its favorite pastime: murdering each other. We even set up a quasi-military club to check their sanguine impulses - NATO. America gets nothing from Europe but an all-Europe museum pass - and they are proving to be rotten stewards of the Western Artifacts Museum at that - it really looks like all the great museum pieces of our heritage are flame-bound. O well.

Muslims: Jew-hatred is the door preventing you from entering the modern world; smash it down.

As for America, we are at the crossroads. We cannot allow anti-Semitism to gain traction in our world. There is a solution, but it will take a bit of work: every politician and then every church, every Synagogue, every mosque in America to denounce anti-Semitism; chase it back into the moral dungeon where it belongs.
