Curt Fey was a significant figure at Xerox, where he worked on research and development strategy from 1970 until 2002. Born in Berlin in 1927, he grew up in Switzerland and moved to the US for his education. He earned degrees from Haverford College, the University of Pennsylvania, and the University of Rochester. After leaving Xerox, he started a small investment advising company, which he retired from in 2016. Fey passed away in 2017, leaving behind a legacy of contributions to both Xerox and the investment field.
