### The Mooch Report—March 3, 2026

Over the weekend, US and Israeli forces launch a coordinated attack on Iranian missile sites, nuclear facilities and key leadership, reportedly killing top officials, including Iran’s supreme leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, IRGC Commander Gen. Mohammad Pakpour, and Defense Minister Amir Nasirzadeh.

We know there was a lot going on for many of our subscribers, so we’ve included both yesterday’s Livestream update, as well as an encore presentation of Mooch’s live stream on Saturday.

As of this writing, the US was 70+ hours into Operation Epic Fury, but Pentagon officials and the administration say the mission will continue. So far, six US service members have been killed in an Iranian missile and drone attacks, and three F-15E Strike Eagles were shot down in an apparent incident of friendly fire. The six crew successfully ejected and were unharmed.
