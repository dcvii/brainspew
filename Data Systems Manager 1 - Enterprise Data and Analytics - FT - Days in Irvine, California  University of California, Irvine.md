**Who We Are**  
  

**UCI Health** is the clinical enterprise of the University of California, Irvine, and the only academic health system based in Orange County. UCI Health is comprised of its main campus, [UCI Medical Center](https://www.ucihealth.org/locations/orange/uci-medical-center), a 459-bed, acute care hospital in in Orange, Calif., four hospitals and affiliated physicians of the UCI Health Community Network in Orange and Los Angeles counties and ambulatory care centers across the region. Listed among America’s Best Hospitals by U.S. News & World Report for 23 consecutive years, UCI Medical Center provides tertiary and quaternary care and is home to Orange County’s only [National Cancer Institute-designated comprehensive cancer center](https://www.ucihealth.org/news/2022/04/uci-cancer-center-renewed-as-nci-comprehensive-center), [high-risk perinatal/neonatal program](https://www.ucihealth.org/medical-services/maternity/high-risk-pregnancy) and American College of Surgeons-verified [Level I adult and Level II pediatric trauma center](https://www.ucihealth.org/medical-services/trauma-critical-care-surgery), [gold level 1 geriatric emergency department](https://www.ucihealth.org/news/2023/06/geriatric-emergency-care) and [regional burn center](https://www.ucihealth.org/medical-services/burn-center). UCI Health serves a region of nearly 4 million people in Orange County, western Riverside County and southeast Los Angeles County.

To learn more about UCI Health, visit [www.ucihealth.org.](https://www.ucihealth.org/)

  
**Your Role on the Team**  
  
**Position Summary:**

The incumbent manages a team that provides the comprehensive Business Intelligence and Analytics (BIA) services for the UCI Medical Center and School of Medicine. Responsible for the development and maintenance of a global enterprise data architecture strategy to guide and inform all initiatives related to the re-use of clinical data to support quality, regulatory, financial, administrative and clinical research. Champion and govern enterprise data architecture standards, tools, best practices and development methodologies as well as provide proactive technical oversight. Mentor BIA developers and analysts fostering re-use, design for scale, performance, stability, and operational efficiency. Deliver solutions in the following areas: analysis, design & implementation of logical data models; physical database objects; data conversion, integration, and loading processes (ETL); query and reporting functions; data management, visualization and governance; and data quality assurance processes

  
**What It Takes to be Successful**  
  
**Required Qualifications:**  
- Strong healthcare informatics knowledge, especially in the area of data management, including data warehouse development and implementation, data integration (ETL), and meta-data management
- Must possess the skill, knowledge and ability essential to the successful performance of assigned duties
- Must demonstrate customer service skills appropriate to the job
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
- BA in Biomedical Informatics, Computer Science or related field
- Ability to translate business questions and requirements into reports, templates and analytical views
- Ability to manage multiple medium to large projects simultaneously
- Ability to maintain a work pace appropriate to the workload
- Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and to analyze and interpret data in order to draw valid conclusions for complex business issues
- 5+ years of programming experience, including SQL, XML and Java
- 5+ years of experience designing, configuring, and managing BI tools that support user-defined needs, creating and delivering user-defined reports, providing end-user training, and documentation in a healthcare environment
- 2+ years of experience managing a team responsible for developing and implementing BI, analytics and data warehousing solutions in a healthcare environment
  
**Preferred Qualifications:**  
- Masters in Biomedical Informatics, Computer Science or related field
- Knowledge of healthcare and academic industry
- Experience using ServiceNow to manage team workload
- Experience using SSIS to build ETLs
- Experience using MS Reporting Services (MSRS) and knowledge of SQL Server Reporting Services
- Data Warehouse development experience
- Business Intelligence (BI) tools experience
