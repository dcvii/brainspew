**Reza Pahlavi** @PahlaviReza [2026-02-27](https://x.com/PahlaviReza/status/2027445822384685058)

Dear Fellow Countrymen,

Today, the updated version of the Emergency Handbook of the Iran Prosperity Project is being released.

The Iran Prosperity Project is the fifth component of our five-part strategy to reclaim Iran and rebuild it. The Prosperity Project provides the vision and roadmap for transitioning to democracy and reconstructing the country.

The Emergency Handbook focuses on the country’s immediate needs in the first six months, the looming crises during this period, and the solutions and opportunities, and has been prepared with the direct participation of dozens of experts. The draft of this handbook was presented in the summer of 1404.

In line with maximum transparency and accountability, experts and the general public were asked to share their views with those involved. The Iran Prosperity Project team spent hundreds of hours in discussions with experts; reviewed thousands of suggestions received, and now presents the updated version.

I am grateful to the management, executive team, authors, and consultants of the Iran Prosperity Project for taking this significant step. I also thank everyone who, with heartfelt and constructive intent, offered their suggestions to improve the quality of this handbook.

For the first time in these past few decades, a national project of this scale has been conducted and brought to fruition with public participation and in a transparent process. We will continue and utilize this process in other phases of the Prosperity Project and in other projects.

The Emergency Handbook outlines the general framework and programs that I support for governing the country in the first six months following the fall of the Islamic Republic, during the transition period. My team and I are committed to these programs and ready to take the reins of affairs after leading the Lion and Sun Revolution to the destination of victory and overthrowing the Islamic Republic. Following that, we will guide and bring to completion the process of power transfer, establishing the transition system, ensuring security, rebuilding the country, and transitioning to democracy.

As always, the support and trust of you, the great nation of Iran, has been and will remain my greatest asset.

Long live Iran,

Reza Pahlavi
