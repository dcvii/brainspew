AI Tinkerers LA: March Builder Meetup & Live Demos

Los Angeles

Attendees rated this event 4.4 / 5 across 7 survey responses. Venue scored 4.1 / 5. Speaker feedback was strong overall, led by Frank Shotwell at 4.5 / 5.
