## Axe-wielding Dundee teen who 'defended friend from migrant man' becomes social media hero

## Police said the 14-year-old girl has been charged and 'will be reported to the relevant authorities' but video footage is going viral, with one person describing the image as 'iconic'

![The incident took place by Coupar Angus Road in Lochee](https://i2-prod.scottishdailyexpress.co.uk/article35797260.ece/ALTERNATES/s1200f/0_Screen-Shot-2025-08-26-at-175239.png)

An axe-wielding schoolgirl from [Dundee](https://www.scottishdailyexpress.co.uk/all-about/dundee) who is said to have been defending a young female from a migrant man has become a social media hero.

The girl, aged 14, has been [charged with possession of a bladed weapon](https://www.scottishdailyexpress.co.uk/all-about/crime) after police were called to the scene in the Lochee district of the city at the weekend.

A video that has been widely circulated on social media shows the youngster brandishing an axe and a machete. In it, she shouts at somebody off camera that they are "f\*\*\*ing battering kids, mate... You're f\*\*\*ing kid bashers".

The footage was taken on Saturday afternoon and shows two girls on a grass verge by the busy Coupar Angus Road. A man's voice is heard saying: "Show the knife, show the knife."

The second girl then screams "Get the f\*\*\* away from her" before the other youngster holds up the two weapons. The second girl shouts: "Don't f\*\*\*ing punch my little sister, she's 12."

It is not entirely clear what happened during the incident, but Police Scotland is understood to be aware of the claims surrounding the motive for her behaviour. As she has been charged, she cannot be identified under Scots Law.

![The girl has been charged ](https://i2-prod.scottishdailyexpress.co.uk/article35788232.ece/ALTERNATES/s1200e/0_Screenshot-2025-08-25-at-110952_censored.jpg)

An X user known as Basil the Great, who hosts a podcast called It's Only Politics, posted the video with the comment: "Little girls scream at a migrant recording them before brandishing an AXE and a KNIFE to warn him off What is happening to our country?"

'Basil' added: "She's a child. A little girl. Someone's daughter. And she's screaming at a grown foreign man to leave her alone. WE SHOULDN'T BE LIVING LIKE THIS!"

Danish 'data scientist' Jonatan Pallesen said: "The more I look at this image, the more I think it has the potential to become iconic. The UK has catastrophically failed in protecting their 12-year-old girls... and so this 14-year-old sister must do it."

A Police Scotland spokesman said: "Around 7.40pm on Saturday, we received a report of a female youth with a bladed weapon in St Ann Lane, Dundee. Officers attended and a 14-year-old girl was charged in connection. She will be reported to the relevant authorities."
